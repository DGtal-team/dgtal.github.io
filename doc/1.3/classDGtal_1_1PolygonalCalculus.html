Detailed Description

template<typename TRealPoint, typename TRealVector>
class DGtal::PolygonalCalculus< TRealPoint, TRealVector >

Implements differential operators on polygonal surfaces from [43].

Description of template class 'PolygonalCalculus'

See Discrete differential calculus on polygonal surfaces for details.

Note
The sign convention for the divergence and the Laplacian operator is opposite to the one of [43]. This is to match the usual mathematical convention that the Laplacian (and the Laplacian-Beltrami) has negative eigenvalues (and is the sum of second derivatives in the cartesian grid). It also follows the formal adjointness of exterior derivative and opposite of divergence as relation \( \langle \mathrm{d} u, v \rangle = - \langle u, \mathrm{div} v \rangle \). See also https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LaplaceBeltrami_operator
